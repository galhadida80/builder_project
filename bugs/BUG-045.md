# BUG-045: No meeting cancellation functionality

## Severity
High

## Category
Meetings

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Create a meeting
2. Look for cancel meeting option
3. Try to cancel upcoming meeting

## Expected Result
Should be able to:
- Cancel meeting
- Notify attendees of cancellation
- Keep record of cancelled meetings
- Add cancellation reason

## Actual Result
No cancel meeting option visible
Users cannot cancel meetings
Attendees not notified of changes
Missing critical meeting management feature
