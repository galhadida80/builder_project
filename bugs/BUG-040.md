# BUG-040: Material quantity cannot be updated after creation

## Severity
High

## Category
Materials

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Create material with quantity 100
2. Material is partially used (20 units)
3. Try to update remaining quantity to 80

## Expected Result
Should be able to:
- Edit material quantity
- Track usage/consumption
- Maintain quantity history
- "Adjust Quantity" feature

## Actual Result
No visible edit functionality
Cannot track material consumption
Cannot adjust for deliveries or usage
Critical inventory management missing
